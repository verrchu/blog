<aside class="callout">

Posts here are often motivated by situations I encounter at work, so the examples reflect that
(orderbooks, market data feeds, trading-bot configuration, and so on). I prefer keeping

equivalent for the sake of neutrality. The framing is incidental; the mechanics of the
examples is what matters.
